#Basotho Cultural festival
##Project aim
To attract tourists and promote the celebration of Basotho culture though an informative website.
home page-overview of the event, highlighting key details andattractions.
about page-information about the festival's purpose, history, and significance.
programe page-activeties and schedule that will take place during the festival.
ticket page-details on ticket purchasing and pricing.
contact-page-way to get more information or reach out for inquiries.
