Welcome to the Basotho Cultural Festival

The Basotho Cultural Fstive is a vibrant celebration of traditions, music, food, and heritage of the Basotho people. The event brings together local communities and international tourists to experience the unique culture of Lesotho.

Join us for a weekend full of traditional dances, local cuisine, art exibition, and live performances. The festival offers visitors the opportunity to explore the beauty and cultural richness of Lesotho.

Event Details

  • Date:10-12 April 2026
  • Location:Morija Cultural Village, Lesotho
  • Time:09:00AM-08:00PM
